# Contributing

Community contributions are welcomed and greatly appreciated. At the project's current stage, the scope of accepted contributions are limited to prioritize solidifying its design and establishing a stable long-term direction.

To ensure that contributions are impactful and aligned with these objectives, contributors are encouraged to focus on the following areas:

- Proposals for improving or refining the API.
- Suggestions for integrating in production projects or real-world usage scenarios.
- Development and improvement of unit tests.
- Improvements to performance.

## Changesets

Changesets pre-v1 should be be denoted as a `minor` or `patch` even if a breaking change is introduced.

## Setup

- Run `npx lefthook install` in the local project root after cloning.
- Commit messages should adhere to the standard provided in the commitlint config.
